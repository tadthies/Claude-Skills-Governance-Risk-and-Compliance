## Prioritized Remediation Plan

**Tier 1 — Critical practices, must be fully MET before any certification (no POA&M eligibility):**
1. **AC.L2-3.1.3 — CUI flow control.** Segment the flat network; move CUI off open shared drives into an access-controlled, ideally isolated enclave. This is the highest-value, highest-effort item and should start immediately — it also reduces your assessment scope if done as a dedicated CUI enclave.
2. **IA.L2-3.5.3 — MFA everywhere.** Extend MFA beyond cloud email to VPN, on-prem AD (including legacy CAD workstations), and all privileged accounts. Legacy CAD workstations on AD may need a bridge solution (e.g., MFA at the VPN/network layer if endpoint-level MFA isn't feasible) — document any compensating control in the SSP.
3. **SC.L2-3.13.11 / SC.L2-3.13.16 — FIPS-validated encryption for CUI at rest.** Deploy FIPS 140-2/3 validated encryption on the shared drives and any other CUI storage location. "AES-256" alone is not sufficient — the module itself must be FIPS-validated.
4. **Verify SC.L2-3.13.8** (in-transit encryption) is also FIPS-validated; this is equally critical and non-POA&M-eligible if not.
5. **IR.L2-3.6.1 — IR capability.** Confirm the plan covers preparation, detection, analysis, containment, recovery, and user response — not just a document, but an operational capability.

**Tier 2 — High-value, POA&M-eligible if not resolved before assessment:**
6. **IR.L2-3.6.3 — Test the IR plan.** Run a tabletop exercise and document it; this also strengthens evidence for 3.6.1.
7. **CA.L2-3.12.4 — Rewrite the SSP.** A 2022 SSP predates most of these gaps and the remediation itself; update it to reflect current systems, the new CUI enclave, MFA scope, and encryption approach as each is completed.
8. **MP.L2-3.8.1/3.8.2 — Media protection policy** covering the shared drives and any removable media.

**Tier 3 — Verify and close:**
9. Confirm least privilege (AC.L2-3.1.5) and external connection controls (AC.L2-3.1.20) once the network is segmented.
10. Formalize offboarding/access-revocation procedures (PS.L2-3.9.2).
11. Confirm insider-threat training content (AT.L2-3.2.3) is explicitly included in the existing annual training.

## Bottom Line

You have three critical, non-negotiable, non-POA&M-eligible gaps (CUI flow control, MFA, FIPS encryption at rest — and likely in transit) that must be fully remediated before certification is possible at all, regardless of your overall score. Even with only the confirmed gaps, your estimated SPRS score (~91) sits below the maximum but likely above the 88-point conditional-certification floor — however, because three of your gaps are on the critical/non-POA&M list, a conditional certification is **not available** until all three are fully MET, independent of the numeric score. Prioritize the Tier 1 items first; everything else can be sequenced around them.
